# Rummy 71 Variant Rules

## Overview
Rummy 71 is a classic card game variant played with 2-6 players using two standard decks of 52 cards (plus jokers if desired). The objective is to form valid sets and runs, and to be the first to discard all cards by placing them on the table with a minimum value of 71 points.

## Setup
- **Players:** 2-6
-- **Deck:** Two standard 52-card decks (Jokers optional)
- **Deal:** Each player is dealt 14 cards. The dealer receives 15 cards and must discard one card to start the game.
- **Stock:** The remaining cards form the draw pile (stock).
- **Discard Pile:** The dealer discards one card to start the discard pile.

## Card Values
- Ace: 1 point if used in a run as 1-2-3; 10 points if used in QKA or AAA sets/runs
- 2-10: Face value
- Jack, Queen, King: 10 points each
- Joker: 0 points (wild card, if used)

## Special Rules for Rummy 71
- The minimum to lay down is 71 points, and this must be achieved without using a joker.
- Each subsequent player who lays down must put down a higher total than the previous player (e.g., if the first player puts down 71, the next must put down at least 72, and so on).
- Players may only pick a card from the center (stock pile) if they are going to lay down cards on the same turn. Otherwise, they must pick from the discard pile.

## Gameplay
1. **Turn Sequence:**
   - Draw a card from the stock or discard pile.
   - Optionally lay down valid sets/runs if you meet the minimum requirement.
   - Discard one card to end your turn.

2. **Sets and Runs:**
   - **Set:** Three or four cards of the same rank (e.g., 7♠ 7♥ 7♦).
   - **Run:** Three or more consecutive cards of the same suit (e.g., 4♣ 5♣ 6♣).
   - Jokers may substitute for any card in a set or run (if used).

3. **Minimum to Lay Down:**
   - You must lay down sets/runs totaling at least 71 points in a single turn to place cards on the table, and this must be done without using a joker.
   - Each subsequent player must lay down more points than the previous player who laid down cards.
   - Until you reach the minimum required points, you cannot lay down any cards.

4. **After Laying Down:**
   - Once you have laid down 71 points, you may add cards to your own or other players' sets/runs in subsequent turns.

## Winning
- The first player to discard all their cards by laying them on the table wins the round.
- Remaining cards in other players' hands count as penalty points.

## Notes
- The game can be played with or without jokers, but the initial 71 points must be achieved without jokers.
- The dealer always starts with 15 cards and discards one to begin play.
- All sets/runs must be valid according to the rules above.
- Players may only draw from the center if they intend to lay down cards that turn.
