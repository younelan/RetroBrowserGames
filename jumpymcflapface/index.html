How to Play

Click, tap, or press Space/Enter to make Jumpy flap his wings!

Click ❤️ to pause, and 📈 to restart.

Obstacles

  • Pipes: Standard vertical obstacles.
  • Spinners: Rotating hazards with gaps.
  • Crushers: Vertical blocks that open and close.
  • Moving Platforms: Platforms that move up and down.
  • Swinging Pendulums: Swinging balls to avoid.

Power-ups

  • 💎: +10 points
  • 🛡️: Temporary shield
  • 🕒: Slows down time
  • 🪶: Shrinks the bird
  • ✖️: Score Multiplier
